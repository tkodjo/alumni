A Global Network.

Supported by the German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD).

Since 2012 the Global and European Studies Institute (GESI) of the University of Leipzig and the Institute for Peace and Security Studies (IPSS) of Addis Ababa University have jointly offered the Master and PhD programmes “Global Studies with a Special Emphasis on Peace and Security in Africa”. The programmes have enrolled students from Cameroon, Djibouti, Egypt, Ethiopia, Ghana, Germany, Nigeria, Rwanda, Uganda, the USA, Tanzania, Zambia, and Zimbabwe giving it a pan-African and international character. Graduates are on their way to hold key positions in the field of peace and security in Africa. This platform is the one-stop-shop for all Global studies alumni united by the experiences of their years in Addis Ababa, Leipzig and their connection to GESI and IPSS.
